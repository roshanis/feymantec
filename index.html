The 5-minute loop

You don't “study” in Feymantec. You teach. The app keeps you moving when you stall.

01

Pick the concept

Type or speak a topic. The app gives you a clean target and a timer.

02

Explain simply

Talk for 2 minutes. No notes. If you reach for jargon, the coach flags it.

03

Find the gap

You get 2–4 sharp questions tied to your own words, not generic trivia.

04

Rewrite + analogy

Compress your explanation. Add one analogy you can remember later.

05

Quick check

Two questions. If you miss, the app schedules a 90-second re-teach.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the demo “real AI”?

Not yet. The iOS app will run a real AI coach and save cards to your profile. This page keeps the demo local so it works anywhere.

What makes it different from “just ask ChatGPT”?

The timer, the gap questions tied to your own explanation, and the habit loop: daily prompts, streaks, review scheduling, and shareable cards that stay short.

Will you support technical + non-technical topics?

Yes. The coach works for math, programming, history, money, and everyday life. The only hard exclusion is NSFW.
