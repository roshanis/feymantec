Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Feymantec ("we," "us," "our") collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Email address — when you join the waitlist or sign in.
  • Explanations and concepts — text you enter into the card generator.
  • Referral data — referral codes you share or use.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, timestamps.
  • Device data — browser type, operating system, screen size.
  • UTM parameters — campaign tracking data from URLs.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Service.
  • To manage your waitlist position and referral rewards.
  • To send you a one-time verification code (email OTP) and product updates.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the user experience.
  • To prevent abuse and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure). We use Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure users can only access their own data. Email verification uses one-time passwords (OTP) — we do not store traditional passwords.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers — infrastructure partners (e.g., Supabase, hosting providers) that help us operate the Service.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, subpoena, or legal process.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

The Service uses browser localStorage and sessionStorage to remember your waitlist status, referral code, and demo state. We do not use third-party tracking cookies. No advertising cookies are used.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured format).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@feymantec.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Waitlist data is retained until the public launch, after which you can request deletion. We may retain anonymized, aggregated data indefinitely for analytics.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

10. International Users

If you are accessing the Service from outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in a different jurisdiction. By using the Service, you consent to such transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@feymantec.com.
